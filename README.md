**Gimmej**
An Infinite Momentum Platformer

**Features**
Dynamic Difficulty: The world speeds up the more Hearts you collect.

**Aerial Glide:** A specialized jump mechanic that allows for precise mid-air navigation.

**Infinite World:** Seamlessly looping terrain for non-stop action.

**How to Play**
Up Arrow: Jump / Activate Glide

Collect: Gather 10 Hearts to spawn the Victory Flag.

Avoid: Do not fall into the water or hit the white hazards!
